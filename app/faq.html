    WHAT IS AN NFT?

    NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token, a one-of-a-kind digital asset that is not interchangeable with another similar item. This means that each of the 10,000 Bohemian Bulldogs has its own set of metadata proving its uniqueness and ownership, which is encrypted on the blockchain. It is a unique digital item that users can buy, own and trade.
    BB's NFTs are created uniquely and exclusively by our artists, all hand-drawn and then registered on the Ethereum blockchain. The NFT contract governing ownership is a standard ERC-721 that works with any compatible service or exchange.
